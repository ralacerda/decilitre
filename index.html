Decilitre

A versatile CSS stylesheet for quick startup, providing a solid foundation while getting out of your way as soon as possible

Quick Start

Link to the Decilitre stylesheets in your HTML <head> tag.

<!-- Decelitre modules -->
<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/decilitre@ 0.6.1 /dist/decilitre.min.css" rel="stylesheet" />
<!-- Optional utilities -->
<link href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/decilitre@ 0.6.1 /dist/utilities.min.css" rel="stylesheet" />
